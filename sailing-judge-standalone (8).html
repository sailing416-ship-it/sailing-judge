How to Get Your API Key — 3 Steps
1
Create a free Anthropic account
Go to console.anthropic.com and sign up. Free to register — no credit card needed.
2
Click "API Keys" then "Create Key"
In the left menu, choose API Keys. Click Create Key. Give it any name. Copy the key — it starts with sk-ant-
3
Paste it above and click Launch
Paste the key above and click Launch Application. The app saves it in this browser — you only need to do this once.
Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Is it free?
Anthropic gives you US$5 of free credit when you sign up — enough for 100–500 queries. Each question costs ~$0.01–$0.05 (1–5 cents Canadian). A full regatta weekend typically costs under $3.
Q: Is my key safe?
Your key is stored only in your browser's local storage on this device. It is sent directly to Anthropic when you ask a question and nowhere else.
Q: Do other judges need their own key?
Yes — each device needs its own key. Share the app link: sailing416-ship-it.github.io/sailing-judge — they open it in any browser and create their own free account.
Q: Can I set a spending limit?
Yes. At console.anthropic.com go to Settings → Limits to set a monthly spending cap.
💡 Tip: The Hearing Wizard, Rules Reference, Sail Canada Prescriptions, Hearing Log and Decision Form all work without an API key. Only the AI Assistant tab requires one.
